## Asset Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with alpha channel
- **Dimensions**: 
  - Mist/Nebula: 1920x1080 or larger (full HD+)
  - Shimmer: 512x512 or larger (square)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (< 300KB per image)
- **Style**: Cosmic, ethereal, aligned with Jyoti.ai brand
- **Colors**: 
  - Mist: Deep purple (#6E2DEB), cosmic violet (#9D4EDD), transparent
  - Gold: Golden yellow (#F2C94C), warm gold (#E6B800)
  - Nebula: Navy (#030014), purple (#6E2DEB), violet (#9D4EDD)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 
  - Mist/Nebula: 16:9 or similar
  - Shimmer: 1:1 (square)
